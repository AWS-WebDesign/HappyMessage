apple picking

the pacific northwest is known for our delicious apples, so why not go apple picking during the fall? below are just two options for this very northwest activity.

Jones Creek Farms

Jones Creek Farms offers more than 50 varieties of u-pick apples from August to October. The 2019 apple picking season is scheduled to kick off Aug. 9-12 and is likely to go through the end of October

Bellewood Acres

One of the largest apple orchards in Western Washington, Bellewood offers u-pick apples, cider press visits, a country cafe, and an apple themed gift shop. They carry 21 apple varieties as well as other produce.
