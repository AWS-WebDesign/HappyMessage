The Iguana is a lizard from:

  1. Mexico
  2. Central America
  3. South America
  4. The Caribbean
Scaley, slow, and not always green, these reptiles can even detach their tails when in danger to make a quick escape.

Fun Facts!

  • Iguanas can vary in size depending on the species. On average, they are usually 6 to 6.5 feet long, weighting 11 pounds. Iguanas are the largest lizards in America
  • Green iguanas have very sharp teeth capable of shredding leaves and puncturing human skin
  • Iguanas can spend 28 minutes under the water without returning to the surface to breathe air.
